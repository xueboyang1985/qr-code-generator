FAQ

Can I change the QR code colors?

Yes. Customize foreground and background colors to match your brand. QR codes remain scannable with high-contrast color combinations.

What is the maximum QR code size?

Free version generates up to 512px. PRO users can generate up to 1024px for high-quality printing.

Are QR codes generated server-side?

No. Everything runs in your browser using the qrcodejs library. Nothing is uploaded.
