What Can You Put in a QR Code?

  • URL — link to any website or landing page
  • WiFi login — generate a QR code that connects phones to your WiFi instantly
  • vCard / Business card — share contact info with a scan
  • Email — pre-filled email with subject line
  • SMS — pre-written text message
  • Phone — dial a number directly
  • Plain text — any text content
